Qualifying journals

An economist is admitted to the catalogue if they have at least one publication in one of the journals below. Once admitted, all of their papers and recent working papers are eligible.

Roughly this is D1 — top general interest — plus the leading field journals in labor, trade, finance, macro, IO, public, development, econometrics, theory, management, urban, health, environment, economic history. About the top 50 economics journals, plus top finance and management outlets.

Any such list is arbitrary. Suggestions welcome via GitHub issue or PR.
